Microsoft Project 15 Open Dataset

Saint Lucia FAD data

Our services provides data around Fish Aggregating Devices (FAD). Big data is a very important concept nowadays, it is used in analysis, machine learning, modeling etc. However, for most people who want to use the convenience of the data, they often have questions -- where and how could I find and use the data? That is why we are here to help!

What We Do About FAD Data

Automatically upload datasets to Azure Storage

Search the data in Azure by keywords or labels

Display the data by maps, graphs or tables

Abstract

Current Problems

Nowadays, critical conservational and sustainability research is conducted for ecosystem monitoring, species tracking, pollution detection. But many of these projects fail due to technology skill gap or development cost. For the implementation of a Fishing Aggregating Device within Saint Lucia, data scientists can't easily find or connect to meaningful data. As a result, end-users can lose FADs in the ocean.

Our Solution

Our solution aims to accelerate the workflow of scientists. It downloads ocean data, processes and parses it into CSV or JSON formats. Datasets are then filtered to be specific to Saint Lucia and loaded into an Azure storage account. From this point, conservationists can connect to it with a few mouse clicks and generate faster analytics, machine learning models, better charts and map visualizations.

Achievement & Impact

For our outputs, information such as FADs' locations or Temperature of FADs can be presented to end-users intuitively, resulting in more efficient usage of data. To give an example, our progressive web app can displays trustworthy analytics and maps visualizations of FADs. By these idiomatically approaches, data scientists are able to perform better when working with data of FADs.
